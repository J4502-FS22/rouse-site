How To Read Photobooks

Do you have a library full of photography books and no idea how to actually incorporate them into your own artistic practice? Well then do I have the guide for you! Looking at and understanding the work that has come before you is a vital part of being in conversation with that work, understanding aesthetic and storytelling methods, and developing your own vision as a photographer. And now, with this super straightforward guide, you’ll know how the greats do it.

(Editor’s note: This guide is a work of satire and not meant to be taken literally. It’s primary purpose is to induce a chuckle and bring some lightheartedness to Tristen’s Photobook Website.)
